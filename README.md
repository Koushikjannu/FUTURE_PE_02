# Task 2 – UGC Ads (Egg Maggi)

## Prompt
Act as a UGC content creator and marketing expert.

Create engaging UGC ad content for a street-style egg maggi food product.

Include:
- 3 viral hooks (attention-grabbing first lines)
- 2 short ad scripts (15–20 seconds, relatable and entertaining)
- 2 captions with emojis and call-to-action

Target audience: young people who love street food and relatable content.

Make it fun, natural, and scroll-stopping.
